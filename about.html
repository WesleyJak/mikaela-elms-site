About Mikaela

Cellist · Educator · Arranger

Mikaela Elms

Mikaela Elms is a cellist, string educator, and music arranger based in Albuquerque, New Mexico. She has spent over a decade immersed in the Albuquerque music scene — as a performing musician, a private studio teacher, and an active presence in the local school orchestra community.

Mikaela holds a Bachelor of Music in Music Performance and has maintained a full-time private cello studio since 2015, teaching students from beginner through advanced levels. Alongside individual lessons, she has coached cello group classes and chamber string ensembles of varying sizes, giving her a breadth of experience across formats and skill levels.

As a performer, Mikaela is a cellist with the Albuquerque Philharmonic Orchestra and has performed at a wide range of events including weddings, corporate concerts, and chamber recitals throughout New Mexico.

Her arranging work grew naturally out of a desire to find the right piece for every student and ensemble — and when the right piece didn't exist, she created it. She now publishes her string arrangements online through SheetMusicPlus, where they are used by players and educators around the world.

Mikaela is currently pursuing alternative teacher licensure and teaches orchestra in the Albuquerque public school district. She has also served as a judge at multiple district-level orchestra competitions.
