About Mikaela

Musician. Educator. Arranger.

Mikaela Elms is a cellist, private studio teacher, and arranger based in Albuquerque, New Mexico. A performer with the Albuquerque Philharmonic Orchestra and a dedicated string educator since 2015, she brings both professional musicianship and a deep love of teaching to everything she creates. Her arrangements are designed to be beautiful to hear and a pleasure to play.
